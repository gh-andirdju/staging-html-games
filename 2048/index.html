Welcome to 2048!

Slide tiles on the board to combine matching numbers. Your goal: create a tile that reaches 2048.

↑←↓→

How to move

Press the arrow keys on desktop, or swipe on mobile. Every tile on the board shifts in that direction at once.

2 2 → 4

Tiles merge!

When two tiles with the same number collide, they combine into their sum. Keep merging to reach higher values.

💡

Think ahead

A new tile appears after every move. The board fills up fast — keep your highest tile in a corner and plan your next move!
