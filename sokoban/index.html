Sokoban — How to play

  • Push every box onto a target ✕ to finish the level — boxes can only be pushed, never pulled.
  • Don't wedge a box into a corner; Undo takes back your last move.
  • Keyboard: Arrows or WASD move · Z or U undoes; Restart in the topbar resets the level.
  • Touch: D-pad below the board moves; the Undo button sits beside it.
