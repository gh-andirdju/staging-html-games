Pinball — How to play

  • Keep the ball in play — bumpers, pegs, slingshots, targets, and rollovers all score; you get 3 balls.
  • Hold Launch to compress the plunger, then release to fire the ball into the table.
  • Keyboard: Z or ← flips the left flipper · X, / or → flips the right · Space launches · P or Esc pauses · R restarts.
  • Touch: Left/Right Flipper buttons below the table flip; hold the Launch button between them to fire.
