Space Invaders — How to play

  • Shoot down the whole alien wave before it reaches your ship — each new wave is faster and meaner.
  • The four green shields soak up shots and slowly crumble; watch for UFOs, dive bombers, and bosses.
  • Keyboard: ← / → or A / D move · Space fires · P or Esc pauses · R restarts.
  • Touch: ◀ / ▶ move and FIRE shoots below the board; Pause and Restart sit underneath.
