Brick Breaker — How to play

  • Bounce the ball to clear every brick — you have 3 lives.
  • Catch falling capsules: E widens the paddle, S slows the ball, L arms lasers, D doubles balls, P adds a life.
  • Keyboard: ← / → or A / D steer the paddle (the mouse works too) · P or Esc pauses · R restarts.
  • Touch: drag the lane below the board to steer the paddle.
