Minesweeper — How to play

  • Reveal every safe cell without hitting a mine — numbers count the mines in the 8 cells around them.
  • Your first click is always safe; click a revealed number with the right flags around it to chord-reveal its neighbors.
  • Desktop: left-click reveals · right-click flags · R restarts. Easy / Normal / Hard buttons change difficulty.
  • Touch: the Reveal / Flag mode buttons below the board choose what a tap does.
