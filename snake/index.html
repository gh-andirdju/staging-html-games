Snake — How to play

  • Steer the snake to eat food — every bite grows you and scores points.
  • Avoid the walls and your own tail; every 5 foods raises the level and speed.
  • Keyboard: Arrows or WASD steer · P or Esc pauses · R restarts.
  • Touch: D-pad below the board steers; Pause and Restart sit in the topbar.
