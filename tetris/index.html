Marathon Tetris — How to play

  • Fill complete horizontal lines to clear them and score — speed rises with your level.
  • Plan ahead with the Next queue, stash a piece in Hold, and watch the level meter.
  • Chain clears for a Combo bonus, and stack back-to-back Tetrises (or T-spins) for an extra reward.
  • Rotate a T into a tight notch to land a T-spin for a big score — modern SRS wall kicks make the twist-ins possible.
  • Clear the whole board in one go for a Perfect Clear — the biggest bonus in the game.
  • Keyboard: ← / → move · Z / X rotate (↑ also rotates) · ↓ soft drops · Space hard drops · C holds · Esc pauses · R restarts.
  • On a phone the board is full-screen — play by touch: drag to move, tap to rotate, drag down to soft drop, flick down to hard drop, swipe up (or tap Hold) to stash.
  • Two players on one keyboard? Play 2-Player Versus →.
