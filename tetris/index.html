Marathon Tetris — How to play

  • Fill complete horizontal lines to clear them and score — speed rises with your level.
  • Plan ahead with the Next queue, stash a piece in Hold, and watch the level meter.
  • Chain clears for a Combo bonus, and stack back-to-back Tetrises for an extra reward.
  • Keyboard: ← / → move · Z / X rotate (↑ also rotates) · ↓ soft drops · Space hard drops · C holds · Esc pauses · R restarts.
  • Touch the board: drag to move, tap to rotate, flick down to hard drop, swipe up to hold.
  • Or use the pads: ↺ ↻ rotate, ◀ ▶ move, ▼ soft drop, ⤓ hard drop, tap Hold to stash.
