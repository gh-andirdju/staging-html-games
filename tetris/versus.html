2-Player Versus — How to play

  • Two players share one keyboard, split at the T / Y seam.
  • P1 (left): A D move · S soft · W / Q rotate · E hold · Left-Shift hard drop.
  • P2 (right): ← → move · ↓ soft · ↑ / . rotate · / hold · Right-Shift hard drop.
  • Clearing 2+ lines sends garbage rows to your opponent; incoming garbage you can cancel by clearing back.
  • Top out and you lose the round. First to 3 rounds wins. Esc pauses both · R restarts the match · Space starts the next round.
