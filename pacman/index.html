Pac-Man — How to play

  • Eat every pellet in the maze to clear the level — avoid the four ghosts.
  • Power pellets turn the ghosts blue; eat them for bonus points before they recover.
  • Keyboard: Arrows or WASD steer · P or Esc pauses · R restarts.
  • Touch: D-pad below the maze steers; Pause and Restart sit beside it.
