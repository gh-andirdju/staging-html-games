Pong — How to play

  • Rally against the AI paddle — first to 7 points wins the match.
  • The ball speeds up on every paddle hit; edge hits deflect at steeper angles.
  • Keyboard: W / S or Arrow Up / Down move your paddle · P or Esc pauses · R restarts.
  • Touch: hold the ▲ / ▼ buttons below the board to move; Pause and Restart sit beside them.
