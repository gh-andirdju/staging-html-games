Asteroids — How to play

  • Blast every asteroid — big rocks split into smaller, faster ones; clear the wave for the next level.
  • You have 3 lives; after a hit you respawn briefly invincible.
  • Keyboard: ← / → rotate · ↑ thrusts · Space fires · P or Esc pauses · R restarts when paused or after game over.
  • Touch: ↺ / ↻ rotate, ▲ thrusts, ● fires — all below the board.
